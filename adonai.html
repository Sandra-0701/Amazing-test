ABOUT OUR CHURCH

Independent Pentecostal Church in Thiruvalla

Adonai Church Worship Centre is one of the independent Spirit-filled churches in Thiruvalla where we expect the manifestation of Jesus Christ through Spirit-filled worship and the Word of God.

Under the leadership of Pastor Thomas, we are committed to spreading the Gospel and experiencing the power of the Holy Spirit in our gatherings.
